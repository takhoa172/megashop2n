## 3. Fix Modal width — Tailwind v4 `max-w-*` bug

**Vấn đề**: Modal, Confirm Dialog, Order success, About page dùng `max-w-lg`/`max-w-sm`/`max-w-md` → trong Tailwind v4 lấy giá trị từ `--spacing-*` (24px/8px/16px).

**Fix**: Thay bằng arbitrary values:
- `max-w-lg` → `max-w-[32rem]` (modal)
- `max-w-sm` → `max-w-[24rem]` (confirm dialog)
- `max-w-md` → `max-w-[28rem]` (about)
- `max-w-lg` → `max-w-[32rem]` (order success)

**Files**:
- `frontend/src/components/ui/modal.tsx`
- `frontend/src/components/ui/confirm-dialog.tsx`
- `frontend/src/app/(public)/order/success/page.tsx`
- `frontend/src/app/(public)/about/page.tsx`
